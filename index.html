About Eman Safavi

Eman Safavi is a student of the master program "Design and Computation" at the Universität der Künste (UDK) and Technische Universität (TU) in Berlin. As an interdisciplinary artist, Eman connects music, technology, and design. He explores the blend of art and technology, creating innovative soundscapes and interactive installations.
